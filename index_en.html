Install Guide (iOS)

  1. Download the app and install.
  2. Setting > General >  Profiles & Device Management -> "KOREAN AIR LINES.CO., LTD." -> "Trust KOREAN AIR LINES.CO., LTD."
  3. Launch the app and sign in with your Hanway account and password.

Install Guide (Android)


  1. Download the install file.
  2. "For security, your phone is set to block…"  -> SETTINGS -> "Unknown sources" ON  -> Allow the installation
  3. Launch the app and sign in with your Hanway account and password
